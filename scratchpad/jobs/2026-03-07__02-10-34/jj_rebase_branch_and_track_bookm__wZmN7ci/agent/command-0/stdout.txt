Your task is to rebase the entire `feature/add-metrics` branch on top of the updated `main` bookmark so the feature work incorporates the latest infrastructure, and then confirm that the `feature/add-metrics` bookmark is correctly tracking the rebased tip. ## Repository State The working directory is `/home/user/monorepo`. After a rebase, bookmarks that pointed to the rebased commits are automatically moved to the corresponding new commits. - You do not need to manually update the bookmark after rebase; jj handles this automatically.
